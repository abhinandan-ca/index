What We Do

Our Professional Services

We offer a full range of chartered accountancy services tailored to the needs of businesses, professionals, and individuals.

01
Income Tax
Filing of income tax returns for individuals, firms, companies, and trusts. Advance tax computation, tax planning, and representation in scrutiny assessments and appeals before CIT(A) and ITAT.
02
TDS Compliance
TDS deduction, deposit, and quarterly return filing (Forms 24Q, 26Q, 27Q, 27EQ). Reconciliation, correction statements, and assistance with demand notices and defaults.
03
Goods & Services Tax
GST registration, GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C filings, ITC reconciliation, e-way bill advisory, and representation before GST authorities.
04
Audit & Assurance
Statutory audit under the Companies Act, tax audit under Section 44AB, internal audit, and concurrent audit assignments for banking and financial institutions.
05
Tax Litigation
Drafting and filing of rectification applications (Section 154), appeals before CIT(A) and ITAT, and GST appellate proceedings including pre-deposit applications before the Appellate Authority.
06
Company Law & ROC
Incorporation of companies and LLPs, annual return and financial statement filings with MCA/ROC, maintenance of statutory registers, and compliance under the Companies Act, 2013.
07
Accounting & Bookkeeping
Maintenance of books of accounts, preparation of financial statements, MIS reporting, and outsourced accounting support for businesses and professional firms.
08
Business Advisory
Advisory on entity structuring, regulatory compliance, financial planning, and management consultancy services for SMEs, traders, and professionals in Punjab.

About the Firm

Sanjiv Vinod and Associates

Sanjiv Vinod and Associates is a Patiala-based chartered accountancy firm providing reliable and comprehensive financial and compliance services to businesses and individuals across Punjab.

The firm is led by CA Vinod Aggarwal and CA Abhinandan Aggarwal, whose combined expertise spans direct taxation, indirect tax, statutory audit, and regulatory compliance. The practice handles matters before income tax authorities, the GST department, and appellate forums.

We are committed to providing professional services with personal attention — keeping clients informed, ensuring timely compliance, and providing clear guidance on matters that affect their financial and legal obligations.
